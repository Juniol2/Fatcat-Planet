    Fat Cat Planet is a community of cat lovers. We have a collection of 3,333 randomly generated NFTs on the Ethereum blockchain. Here, you can have interactions with pet lovers from all over the globe and on every journey you take , you will also be part of our mission which is dedicated to helping stray and homeless animals. With this kind help from every member , a portion of the proceeds will be used to purchase food and go towards vet bills for stray cats and other animals.

  

How did the Fat Cat Planet get started?

    The beginning of Fat Cat is a coincidence when the project's owner attended a pet fair and found the booth of Homeless Animals Foundation with stray rescues in the cage. He got the idea to help those stray animals by building up a pet community where anyone can gather and start a NFT journey. So we can make donations to the Homeless Animals Foundation from the NFT collection therefore empowering every member to be part of it.

  

How many NFTs will be minted?

Fat Cat Planet will comprise of 3,333 unique NFTs. Ownership of these NFTs will forever be provable on the Ethereum blockchain and the NFTs will not expire. There will never be more than 3,333 NFTs in the Fat Cat Planet collection.

  

Can I trade my Fat Cat Planet NFT?

Yes. You can buy or sell your NFT on secondary marketplaces such as OpenSea, Nifty Gateway or Foundation.
